# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

DogWalker is committed to a respectful, constructive, and inclusive community.
Contributors, maintainers, and participants in project spaces are expected to:

- Be respectful in discussions, reviews, and feedback
- Assume good intent while still being clear and technically rigorous
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, and decisions rather than people
- Welcome different viewpoints and lived experiences
- Help keep collaboration professional, useful, and safe

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior is not acceptable in project spaces:

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, inflammatory comments, or repeated bad-faith arguments
- Sharing private or sensitive information without permission
- Sexualized language, imagery, or unwanted advances
- Disruptive behavior that makes collaboration harder for others

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to:

- GitHub issues, pull requests, reviews, and discussions
- Project chat, email, and any other community spaces tied to this repository

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact
`20088349@mail.wit.ie`.

When possible, include:

- What happened
- Where it happened
- Links or screenshots if relevant
- Any immediate safety or moderation concerns

Reports will be reviewed in good faith and handled as privately as practical.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues,
or contributions that violate this code of conduct. Depending on severity,
responses may include a warning, temporary restriction, or permanent removal
from project participation.
